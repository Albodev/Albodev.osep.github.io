EarthDay Writing Competition!

We are pleased to announce the call for Articles for the Earth Day Celebration on 22nd April 2020.We hope to showcase the best articles from across the continent. Let’s all make Earth Day celebration an everyday celebration. Cheers!!

We all acknowledge Earth as our Mother Home! We all acknowledge the sacrifice she makes to keep us alive. We all acknowledge the adversity we all face at the moment. And we all acknowledge the readjustments and renewals our planet is struggling to make so you and me can live.


Why don’t we protect it??


Do you know the threats the planet earth is currently facing? And mark you! They are all human orchestrated. According to Nil Zimmermann (2016) the five world’s biggest environmental nightmares include:

✔ Species Extinction: the IUCN’s red list of threatened species keeps increasing at an alarming rate.

✔ Deforestation: Over 18 million acres of forest are destroyed yearly.

✔ Pollution and Climate Change: Atmospheric CO2 concentrations has moved to400ppm today.

✔ Soil degradation: Over 12 million hectares of farmland are degraded yearly.

✔ Overpopulation: The earth’s resources are overstrained. Additionally, the Demographic projection indicates global population will be 10 Billion by 2050.

Indeed, there is need for Climate Action. How can you contribute towards this venture?


This April 22nd 2020, the World marks the 50TH Earth Day Celebration. This is a special day when we all recognize the great contribution of our mother Earth towards our survival. We laud her unmatched sacrifice to protect humanity and gifting us a comfy space to live.


How are you going to etch this day in our history amidst the COVID-19 Global pandemic? Would you wish your voice heard? You can participate in OSEP Earth Day 2020 Article Competition.


The requested articles are supposed to be relevant to the topics below:

✔ Environmental justice and the role of civil society in sustainability.

✔ Challenges and opportunities of COVID-19 to achieving sustainability in Africa.

Article specifications:

✔ The articles should comprise of 700-1500 words

✔ Articles must be submitted in Ms word /PDF format for ease of review.

✔ Indicate which of the aforementioned topics you will be addressing.

All submissions should be sent to info@osepke.orgbefore 5TH May 2020. Announcements of winning submissions shall be made on our Facebook, Twitter and website www.osepke.org.


The best three articles in each category will be published in our website and online platforms


All participants shall be given Certificate of Recognition “OSEP EARTH DAY CONTEST 2020”


We hope to receive your articles for this competition.


Best wishes!!!
